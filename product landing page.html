TRS is the maker of India's most reliable pump brands, TEXMO and AQUATEX.

With over 60 years of experience, TRS hosts a wide range of water pumps for the agricultural, commercial and domestic sectors. We are an award winning manufacturer with captive foundry, motor lamination, and winding wire units. Manufactured in Coimbatore, our TEXMO borewell submersible pumps are India's most preferred due to their consistent quality and robust design.

    DOMESTIC PUMPS


    TRS SUBMERSIBLE PUMPSETS are easy to install, noise free, efficient and require minimum maintenance. A wide range of pumps are available to meet diverse operating requirements.     1.Water extraction from bores of diameter 3″ (80mm) and above
    2.Water supply to commercial establishments like Hotels, Lodges and Hospitals
    3.Domestic water supply to large residential and multi-storied apartments
    4.Garden irrigation including Drip & Sprinkler systems
    5.Pressure boosting applications
